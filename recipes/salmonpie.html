Grandmas Salmon, egg, and rice pie

An instant family favorite consisting of smoked salmon, rice, vegetables, and egg.

Baked in the oven until flakey and savory, perfect to be served with a white sauce or even just plain ol ketsup.

Ingredients

  • Just ask grandma

Steps

  1. Go to the grocery and buy all the ingredients as Grandma advised.
  2. Ask Grandma to make it because no matter how well you do, it will never taste right.
  3. Enjoy your pie, make sure to eat it all as no one else will save you leftovers.
  4. Maybe give Grandma a piece and a kiss. Maybe just the kiss, the pie is just that damn good.
