Cottage Pie

A pie consisting of mince beef, vegetables, and gravy. Baked under a fluffy topping of mashed potatoes.

Creating a filling by browning meat and vegetables, layed into a pie base, then covered with mashed potatoes. this dish will be baked a golden brown and served piping hot.

Ingredients

  • Mince
  • Carrots
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Potatoes

Steps

  1. Brown Mince
  2. Add vegetables and cook until soft
  3. Chop potatoes and add to boiling water or steamer and cook until soft
  4. Place filling into pie tin
  5. Mash potatoes until smooth
  6. Scoop potatoes over filling and cover completly
  7. Back pie until top is golden brown
